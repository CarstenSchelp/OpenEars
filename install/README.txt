Here you will find the different installation options, ordered per platform.

At the moment only the Raspberry Pi 3 is supported, with a variety of microphones, from the Umik to the Trust USB microphone and the AIY sound hats

In checkpoints you will find some tools to test your installation:
-which version of tensorflow is running in your environment (tensorflowversion.sh)
-if you can record sound properly according to the standard required by the platform
